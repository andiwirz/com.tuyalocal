Twelve built-in drivers cover the most common Tuya smart home device types — from air conditioners, smart plugs and lights to garage doors, heat pumps and curtain motors. Each driver automatically detects the device's data points at pairing time and maps them to the right Homey capabilities, so most devices are ready to use straight away. A generic driver is included for any device type not covered by the dedicated drivers.

All communication runs directly over your local Wi-Fi network. Devices respond instantly to Homey commands and push state changes in real time, even without an internet connection. Every driver comes with a full set of Flow cards — triggers, conditions and actions — tailored to its device type, so you can build automations the moment your device is added.
